Starlight Engravings

Category: Dream Journal
Role: Producer & Designer
Team Members: Hyeyoung Park
Date: 2019.11~2019.12
Supporter: Crowdfunding
Website: https://www.tumblbug.com/soulmark

Designed a dream journal inspired by gaufré edges from 16th century German bookbinding techniques using 3D laser engraving machines. The 'starry night' sky patterns 'engraved' on the edges resemble Vincent van Gogh's paintings and is a metaphor of how dreams may hold the artchetypal patterns that are engraved in our psyches or 'souls'. According to James Hillman's acorn theory, each individual life contains a specific life pattern within its seed/soul, a pattern which gestates, then, slowly, evolves into a mature specimen of its species.
